Grand Bazaar (Kapalıçarşı)

One of the world's oldest and largest covered markets, the Grand Bazaar is a bustling shopping destination in the heart of Istanbul. It is located away from the shoreline and at a higher elevation, making it less prone to direct impact from a 10-meter increase in water level.

Süleymaniye Mosque

This historical mosque, built during the Ottoman Empire, is located on the Third Hill of Istanbul. It is situated at a relatively higher elevation compared to the coastline and may not be directly affected by a 10-meter rise in water level.

Chora Church (Kariye Museum)

Situated in the Edirnekapı neighborhood of Istanbul, the Chora Church is renowned for its Byzantine mosaics and frescoes. While it is located closer to the coast, it is positioned on higher ground, making it less susceptible to immediate impact from a 10-meter increase in water level.
